About me

Idaly is a graduate student at the Parsons Laboratory in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, and is also a Senior Research Engineer at the Center of Environmental Sensing and Modeling, Singapore-MIT Alliance for Research and Technology. He is integrally involved in designing, assembling and deploying sensor systems to quantify carbon, water and energy fluxes in tropical peatlands of Borneo, and his professional expertise includes project management, machine learning and data analytics.
